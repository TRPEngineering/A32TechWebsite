What's inside

Everything you'd keep in your back pocket, in one place.

01 / ECAM

ECAM Resets

Fully searchable reset procedures with integrated CB locations, reset timers, and tappable panel images so you can see exactly where the breaker is — without hunting through a diagram folder. No-go faults are clearly flagged throughout.

02 / PARTS

Parts Catalogue

Build your own catalogue of go-to and hard-to-find part numbers. Each entry supports manual references and a photo of the part. Back up and restore your catalogue, or swap it with another engineer — their years of collected part numbers become yours instantly. Duplicate detection keeps it clean.

03 / REFS

My References

Your own personal reference list — quick sign-off notes, AMM callouts, anything you reach for regularly. Works on the same backup and restore principle as the Parts Catalogue so nothing gets lost.

04 / DISPATCH

MEL Calculator

Enter the date of discovery and the app returns the exact expiry for each MEL category — Cat D running 120 days, through to Cat A. No mental arithmetic, no calendar counting.

05 / TOOLS

Converters

Pressure, torque, temperature, and weight converters plus a fuel uplift calculator. The ones you need on the job without digging through a performance manual.

06 / ELEC

CB Locations

Full list of circuit breakers with FIN and physical location. Each entry links to a tappable image of the relevant VU panel so you can see exactly where to look.

07 / STRUCT

Panel Fastener Limits

Search any panel number and get its missing-fastener limits, AMM reference, and corrective action. Also includes a fully browseable panel map — tap any panel on the image and it pulls up everything for that panel directly.

08 / EXTERIOR

Antennas & Lighting

Browseable images for locating every antenna and external light on the aircraft. Search by name and the result isolates that item — everything else drops away.

09 / STRUCT

Structures

Reference images covering zones, frames, stringers, access panels, and VU panels across A320, A321, and A321 XLR.

10 / DISPATCH

CDL Limits

Dispatch limits for missing external parts with MCDL reference. Know what can go and what the requirement is before the crew asks.

11 / FLUIDS

Leakage Rates

Acceptable leakage limits by system for quick on-the-job referencing. The kind of thing that lives on a scrap of paper in most toolboxes.

12 / AMM

Deactivation Tasks

AMM task references for system deactivations with the associated MMEL requirements alongside. Everything in one place for deferred defect management.

13 / AMM

General References

A curated list of high-use AMM references — the chapters and tasks that come up often enough to be worth having at your fingertips.

14 / MCDU

Alpha Callups

A reference list of the parameters you can bring up on the MCDU, with the relevant information for each. Saves you remembering the exact sequence when you need it in a hurry.

15 / GLOSSARY

Acronyms

Aviation and Airbus-specific acronym definitions, searchable. The one you can never remember when someone's watching.
