For reference and training purposes only. A32F Tech is not approved maintenance data. Every licensed engineer is trained and required to use only approved data — AMM, MEL, MMEL, CDL, SRM, TSM, and company procedures — for aircraft work. This app does not change that, and is not held out as a substitute for it. Use it for revision, training, and as a reference companion between tasks.

What's inside

Everything you'd keep in your back pocket, in one place.

Free

No subscription required

01 / ECAM

ECAM Resets

Browse reset procedures for free. Pro unlocks full search, NOGO fault flagging, a dedicated no-reset-available list, bookmarks, reset timers, tappable CB panel images, and direct links to the relevant MMEL entry.

02 / GLOSSARY

Acronyms

Aviation and Airbus-specific acronym definitions, searchable. The one you can never remember when someone's watching.

03 / MCDU

Alpha Callups

FDIU and SDAC parameter monitoring codes — the full list of parameters you can bring up on the MCDU, with the relevant information for each. Saves you remembering the exact sequence when you need it in a hurry.

04 / ELEC

CB Locations

Full list of circuit breakers with FIN and physical location. Each entry links to a tappable image of the relevant VU panel so you can see exactly where to look.

05 / DISPATCH

MEL Calculator

Shows the current expiry date for Cat B, C, and D automatically — no input needed. Cat A is the only one you enter manually, since the time limit is operator-defined. No calendar counting, no mental arithmetic.

06 / EXTERIOR

Antennas & Lighting

Browseable images for locating every antenna, probe, sensor, and external light on the aircraft. Search by name and the result isolates that item — everything else drops away.

07 / AVIONICS

Avionics Computers

Interactive SVG diagrams of the 80VU, 90VU, 105/106/109VU, and Aft Cargo computer bays. Tap any component to highlight it on screen — the fastest way to locate an avionic computer on the aircraft.

08 / DISPATCH

MCDL Limits

Dispatch limits for missing external parts with MCDL reference. Know what can go and what the requirement is before the crew asks.

09 / SYNC

Data Sync

Pull the latest procedure and reference data directly to the app without waiting for a store update. One-way and outbound only — no data ever leaves your device. Can be disabled in settings at any time.

10 / AMM

Deactivation Tasks

AMM task references for system deactivations with direct links to the associated MMEL entry alongside. Everything in one place for deferred defect management.

11 / FLUIDS

Leakage Rates

Acceptable leakage limits grouped by system for quick on-the-job referencing. The kind of thing that lives on a scrap of paper in most toolboxes.

12 / MMEL

MMEL Reference

Full Master Minimum Equipment List, ATA grouped and colour-coded by category — Cat A through D. Tap any deferred defect in an ECAM reset and jump straight to the relevant MMEL entry. Everything you need for a deferred defect in one place.

13 / DOCS

My Documents

Import and store your own PDFs and manuals directly in the app. Native PDF rendering, swipe between images, and sort by date, name, or type. Swipe any document to share it directly to any app on your device or delete it. Document titles are included in the universal library search. Backed up and restored separately from your Parts and Refs library.

14 / NOTES

My Notepad

A freeform scratchpad that lives in the app. Jot down shift notes, snags, or anything you need to remember. Full-screen editor, auto-saves on close, swipe to delete. Included in backup and restore.

15 / REFS

My References

A single fully editable reference library, pre-populated with standard AMM maintenance task references and searchable from the first launch. Add your own entries, edit existing ones, or delete what you don't need. Filter by type: AMM, TSM, SRM, or GEN.

16 / STRUCT

Panel Fastener Limits

Search any panel number and get its missing-fastener limits, AMM reference, and corrective action. Browse diagrams cover the belly, horizontal stabiliser, and vertical stabiliser — tap any panel on the diagram and it pulls up the limits directly.

17 / PARTS

Parts Catalogue

Build your own catalogue of go-to and hard-to-find part numbers. Each entry supports IPC/CMM references, notes, and a photo of the part. Tag by fleet type — including multi-fleet tagging for parts that span variants. Back up and restore your catalogue, or share it with another engineer instantly.

18 / STRUCT

Structures

Access panel, frame, and stringer reference across A320, A321, and A321 XLR. Fuselage panel diagrams include horizontal and vertical stabiliser surfaces with SVG highlighting — tap any panel ID to identify it on screen.

19 / TOOLS

Calculator Library

Eleven aviation-specific calculators, each with its own full-screen view — cable tension, fuel uplift, oil consumption, pressure, rivet layout, sheet metal bend allowance, temperature, torque, torque wrench extension, weight, and wire voltage drop. All formulas verified against published sources including AC 43.13-1B. Reorder and show or hide calculators to match your workflow.

20 / AVIONICS

VU Panels

Full overview and bay-by-bay reference for every VU panel on the aircraft. Navigate from the aircraft overview to any individual panel and see exactly what's installed — from 6VU to 126VU.
