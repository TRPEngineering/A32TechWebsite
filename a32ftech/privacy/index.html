1. Who we are

A32F Tech and MEL Calculator (each an "App", together the "Apps") are published by TRP Engineering Limited, a private company limited by shares incorporated in Ireland, with its registered office in Ireland ("we", "us", "our").

For the purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2016/679, "GDPR") and the Irish Data Protection Act 2018, we are the data controller for any personal data processed in connection with the Apps.

You can contact us about this policy at contact@trpengineering.ie.

2. Summary

We have built both Apps to do their job without collecting personal data. You do not need an account. Both Apps work fully offline. A32F Tech Pro subscribers can optionally enable a one-way data sync to receive procedure and reference updates — no user data is sent or returned by this request. We do not run advertising, tracking, or third-party analytics inside either App.

The only personal data that exists in connection with the Apps is:

  • Subscription billing data (A32F Tech only), processed by Google (Google Play Billing) and Apple (App Store) and by our subscription management provider, RevenueCat. We do not see your payment details.
  • One-time purchase data (MEL Calculator only), processed by Google Play Billing. We do not see your payment details and we do not use RevenueCat for this app.
  • Support correspondence, if you choose to email us.

The sections below explain this in detail and set out your rights under GDPR.

3. What we collect, why, and on what legal basis

3.1 Information processed inside the Apps

No personal data. Neither App collects, stores, or transmits any personally identifiable information. Reference content (A32F Tech) and the MEL date calculation logic (MEL Calculator) are bundled with each App and run locally. We do not operate advertising or tracking SDKs inside them.

A32F Tech performs three types of network activity, all limited in scope:

  • Data sync (Pro subscribers only). A one-way outbound fetch from our backend to download updated procedure and reference data. No user data is included in or returned by this request. Can be disabled at any time in Settings.
  • Push notifications (optional). If you grant notification permission, an anonymous device token is stored on our backend solely to deliver notifications to your device. See section 3.7.
  • Anonymous issue reports (voluntary). See section 3.6.

MEL Calculator never contacts our servers.

All user-created data (My Parts, My Refs, bookmarks, notepad entries, imported documents, and any attached photos) is stored exclusively on your device using hardware-backed AES encryption and is never transmitted. Any preferences you set (dark mode, default aircraft variant) are stored locally only. MEL Calculator saves no preferences and no history.

3.2 Subscription data (A32F Tech)

If you start a free trial or subscribe to a paid plan, the transaction is handled by Google Play Billing. Google processes your payment information under its own privacy policy. We never receive your payment card details.

To manage your subscription entitlement, we use RevenueCat, Inc. as a sub-processor. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app-generated user identifier, purchase receipt data from Google Play, and basic device metadata (platform, app version, country code, language). RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or payment details. Their privacy policy is at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.

Legal basis: performance of a contract (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR).

Retention: purchase records are retained for as long as you have an active entitlement and for up to 7 years after the last transaction, to comply with Irish tax and accounting law (Section 886 Taxes Consolidation Act 1997).

3.3 One-time purchase data (MEL Calculator)

If you purchase MEL Calculator on Google Play, the transaction is handled by Google Play Billing. Google processes your payment information under its own privacy policy. We never receive your payment card details.

MEL Calculator does not use RevenueCat or any third-party subscription management service. We receive only the aggregate sales and country data that Google provides through the Play Console.

Legal basis: performance of a contract (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR).

Retention: purchase records are retained for up to 7 years after the transaction, to comply with Irish tax and accounting law (Section 886 Taxes Consolidation Act 1997).

3.4 Support correspondence

If you email contact@trpengineering.ie, we will receive your email address and the contents of your message. We use this only to respond to you and to keep a record of the issue.

Legal basis: legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR).

Retention: support emails are retained for up to 24 months from the last message in the thread, then deleted.

3.5 Google Play Console, App Store Connect diagnostics, and Firebase

Google Play and Apple's App Store each collect certain technical data (crashes, ANRs, install counts, country, device model) and provide it to us in aggregated form through the Play Console and App Store Connect respectively. This applies to both Apps where they are distributed on the relevant store. This is governed by Google's and Apple's own privacy policies and we have no control over the collection.

A32F Tech also uses Firebase (Google LLC) for passive crash reporting and performance diagnostics. Firebase may collect device model, OS version, and anonymous crash traces to help us identify and fix stability issues. No personal data is linked to crash reports, and we do not use Firebase for advertising or user tracking. Firebase is governed by Google's privacy policy.

3.6 Anonymous issue reports (A32F Tech only)

A32F Tech includes an optional issue reporting feature that allows you to flag errors in reset procedures or CB location data. Submission is voluntary and requires no account.

The report contains: the section type (e.g. ECAM reset, CB location), a description of the affected entry, the type of problem, and optionally the aircraft type and a suggested correction. None of these fields contain or require personally identifiable information.

Reports are transmitted to our API over HTTPS and are not retained on your device after submission. We do not link reports to individual users, devices, or any other data we hold. No new third-party SDKs or processors are involved.

Legal basis: legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) — maintaining accurate reference data benefits all users of the App.

Retention: report data is retained for as long as it is needed to investigate and correct the reported issue, then deleted.

3.7 Push notifications (A32F Tech only — optional)

If you grant notification permission, the app registers an anonymous device token (provided by Expo's push notification service) and stores it on our backend solely for the purpose of delivering notifications to your device.

This token is not linked to your name, email address, subscription identity, or any other personal information. It is not shared with any third party. If you revoke notification permission or uninstall the app, the token becomes inactive.

Legal basis: legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) — delivering relevant operational notifications to users who have explicitly opted in.

Retention: tokens are retained for as long as they are active. Inactive tokens are purged periodically.

3.8 Part photos (A32F Tech only — optional)

When saving a part entry in My Parts, you can optionally attach a photo taken with your camera or selected from your photo library. Photos are compressed and stored locally on your device only. They are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any third party. You can remove any attached photo at any time from within the app.

The app accesses your camera or photo library only when you explicitly initiate this action. No photos are accessed passively or in the background.

Legal basis: consent — you initiate the action each time.

3.9 Imported documents and images (A32F Tech only — optional)

My Documents allows you to import PDFs and images from your device storage or photo library into the app. Imported files are stored exclusively on your device and are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any third party.

The app accesses your device storage or photo library only when you explicitly choose to import a file. No files are accessed passively or in the background. You can delete any imported document at any time from within the app.

The Documents backup feature exports your imported files as a ZIP archive stored to a location of your choosing on your device. This file is not uploaded anywhere by the app.

Legal basis: consent — you initiate each import action.

4. What we do not do

We do not:

  • Sell or rent personal data to anyone.
  • Run advertising or share data with ad networks.
  • Use third-party analytics or tracking inside the Apps.
  • Track your location.
  • Access your contacts, microphone, or location.
  • Access your camera, photo library, or device storage for any purpose other than attaching a photo to a part entry in My Parts (section 3.8) or importing a file into My Documents (section 3.9) — both A32F Tech only, both user-initiated. All files are stored locally and never transmitted.
  • Profile users or make automated decisions producing legal effects.
  • Transfer data outside the EEA except where Google and RevenueCat (US-based) process subscription data on our behalf, under standard contractual clauses and applicable safeguards.

5. Children

Both Apps are professional tools for licensed aircraft engineers. They are not directed at children and are rated for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18.

6. International transfers

Where Google or RevenueCat process personal data outside the European Economic Area (typically in the United States), they do so under the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses and any additional safeguards required under the GDPR. You can request copies of the relevant safeguards from us at contact@trpengineering.ie.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or port personal data we hold about you; and to object to processing based on legitimate interests.

In practice, for the vast majority of users we hold no personally identifiable data at all. All app data (My Parts, My Refs, bookmarks) lives on your device and we cannot access it. Issue reports are anonymous by design — we have no way to link a report back to an individual. If you have never contacted us by email, there is nothing we hold that could identify you.

The only personal data TRP Engineering Limited could hold about a specific individual is support correspondence. If you have emailed us and want that deleted, email contact@trpengineering.ie and we will action it promptly.

For billing and subscription data, that is held by Google and RevenueCat under their own privacy policies — rights requests relating to payment or purchase records should be directed to them.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission (dataprotection.ie) or with the supervisory authority in your country of residence.

8. Security

We use commercially reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, including encryption in transit for any data exchanged with Google and RevenueCat, and access controls on our internal systems. The volume and sensitivity of data we hold is deliberately minimal.

All user-created data stored on-device by A32F Tech (My Parts, My Refs, bookmarks) is protected using hardware-backed AES encryption provided by the device's operating system. The app does not handle or store payment information at any point — all billing is processed by Google Play Billing via RevenueCat.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified through the relevant App and on our website at trpengineering.ie/a32ftech/privacy. The "Last updated" date at the top shows when it was last changed.
